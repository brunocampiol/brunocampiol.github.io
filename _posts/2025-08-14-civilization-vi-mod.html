Civilization VI Raze Cities (and capitals) mod


Hi everyone,

Epic Games continues to impress. Recently, they offered Civilization VI Platinum Edition for free. As one of my favorite turn-based games, I redeemed it immediately. This wasn’t my first experience with the game—I’ve owned the standard version for quite some time and remembered some mods I used to enjoy.

Finding mods through Steam is easier thanks to the Workshop, which handles installation automatically. Unfortunately, this feature isn’t available on Epic Games. While searching for alternatives, I discovered the NexusMods website and found a mod that seemed to offer what I was looking for (raising any city). However, the mod wasn’t working. I decided to try modifying it myself, and after a few attempts, I got it working.

If you’re interested, you can check it out on the NexusMods webpage or in this GitHub repository.
